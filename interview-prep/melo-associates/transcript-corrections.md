# Loom transcript corrections

Strategy: fix what would embarrass or confuse a reviewer (proper nouns, factual errors, technical term mistakes, basic grammar). Leave the small verbal stumbles intact, because a transcript that is too clean signals heavy AI cleanup and reads as fake.

A real speaker has verbal slips. A reviewer who notices a few natural stumbles will trust the transcript more, not less.

1. Click `Correct` button on the transcript tab in Loom.

3. Then the "Should fix" sections.
4. Save. Move on.
5. Do NOT fix the "Skip" section. They are doing work for you by signaling this is a real recording.
