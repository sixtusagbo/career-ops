### 1. Mercor — mercor.com
- $70–200+/hr for senior engineering review tasks.
- One 20-min AI video interview, reused across opportunities.
- Pays via Stripe to 100+ countries incl. Nigeria.

### 2. Proxify — career.proxify.io
- $35–55/hr. FT-style remote contracts (long engagements).
- 2–3% accept rate: timed coding test + live tech interview.
- Anchor your rate at $45/hr in the application.
- Matches fast (~2 days) after acceptance.

### 3. Andela Talent Network — talent.andela.com/signup
- Nigeria is their home market — natural fit.
- $50–100/hr client-side; 12-month-plus engagements.
- Slower to first gig (2–8 weeks) but stable.

### 4. Turing — turing.com (backup for 1)
- $30–80/hr. Confirmed Nigerian devs on platform.
- 1–4 week vetting; bench model — hired once, matched continuously.
